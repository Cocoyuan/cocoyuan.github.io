Brief

During my stay at Royal Philips Design, I was part of the design team of Image Guided Therapy Devices, working on the latest version of the IntraSight solution with new integrated features.

The Intrasight applications platform is where imaging, physiology, co-registration and software all come together to clearly identify coronary and peripheral artery disease, and allow for more optimized treatment plans. It can work across the platform device, the mobile solution, and the touchable product. In addition, the latest Intrasight can be embedded together with other health solutions from Philips in the Azurion system.

My deliverables includes experience maps, user scenarios, concept wireframe, hi-fi UI elements and screens, testable mockups, etc(by using Sketch, Principle, Adobe Suites, Philips own prototype softwares and so on). Besides the key projects, I also supported the advanced development team for mockups, run usability testing and user research sessions. Due to the confidential reason, I could not show the details on product screens or any features we created. Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions and take a look on the Philips website about the relevant existing solutions:

  • Image Guided Therapy
  • IntraSight solution
  • Azurion system
